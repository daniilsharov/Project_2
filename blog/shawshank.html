About the Movie

Title: The Shawshank Redemption

Release Year: 1994

Director: Frank Darabont

Starring: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman

IMDb Rating: 9.3/10

Synopsis

The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 film adapted from Stephen King's novella. It follows Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongfully convicted of his wife's murder, as he spends 25 years in Shawshank State Penitentiary. There, he forms a deep friendship with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding, played by Morgan Freeman.

Throughout the years, Andy uses his intelligence and skills to improve the prison and help fellow inmates, all while secretly planning his escape. The movie is known for its themes of hope, friendship, and redemption, and remains a classic in cinematic history.
