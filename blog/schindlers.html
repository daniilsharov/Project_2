About the Movie

Title: Schindler's List

Release Year: 1993

Director: Steven Spielberg

Starring: Liam Neeson, Ben Kingsley, Ralph Fiennes

IMDb Rating: 9.0/10

Synopsis

Schindler's List is a 1993 historical drama directed by Steven Spielberg, based on the true story of Oskar Schindler. Schindler, a German businessman, saved the lives of over 1,000 Jewish refugees during the Holocaust by employing them in his factory. The film explores Schindler's transformation from a profit-driven opportunist to a humanitarian who risks his own life to protect others from the horrors of the Nazi regime.

The film is renowned for its powerful storytelling, emotional depth, and stark portrayal of the Holocaust. It is considered one of the greatest films ever made, winning seven Academy Awards, including Best Picture and Best Director.
