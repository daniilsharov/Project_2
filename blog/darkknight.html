About the Movie

Title: The Dark Knight

Release Year: 2008

Director: Christopher Nolan

Starring: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart

IMDb Rating: 9.0/10

Synopsis

The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film that continues the story of Bruce Wayne, also known as Batman. In this film, Batman faces off against his most dangerous enemy, the Joker, a criminal mastermind played by Heath Ledger in an iconic performance that won him a posthumous Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor.

As Batman grapples with his role as Gotham's protector, he must confront the chaos unleashed by the Joker, who seeks to plunge the city into anarchy. The movie delves into themes of morality, justice, and the fine line between good and evil.
