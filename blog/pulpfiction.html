About the Movie

Title: Pulp Fiction

Release Year: 1994

Director: Quentin Tarantino

Starring: John Travolta, Uma Thurman, Samuel L. Jackson

IMDb Rating: 8.9/10

Synopsis

Pulp Fiction is a 1994 neo-noir crime film written and directed by Quentin Tarantino. The film's nonlinear narrative intertwines multiple storylines centered around crime and redemption, following the lives of various Los Angeles characters, including hitmen, a gangster's wife, and a boxer caught in a dangerous web.

The film is known for its sharp dialogue, iconic characters, and unique structure. It became a cultural touchstone and is widely regarded as one of the greatest films of all time, earning several Academy Award nominations and wins, including Best Original Screenplay for Tarantino.
