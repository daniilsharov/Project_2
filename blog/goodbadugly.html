About the Movie

Title: The Good, the Bad and the Ugly

Release Year: 1966

Director: Sergio Leone

Starring: Clint Eastwood, Lee Van Cleef, Eli Wallach

IMDb Rating: 8.8/10

Synopsis

The Good, the Bad and the Ugly is a 1966 Spaghetti Western film directed by Sergio Leone. The film follows three gunslingers – Blondie (Clint Eastwood), Angel Eyes (Lee Van Cleef), and Tuco (Eli Wallach) – as they search for a hidden cache of gold. Set against the backdrop of the American Civil War, their journey is fraught with double-crosses, betrayals, and intense showdowns.

Known for its iconic music by Ennio Morricone, this film is widely regarded as one of the greatest Westerns of all time and helped cement Clint Eastwood's status as a Hollywood icon. It also brought about the rise of Spaghetti Westerns as a genre.
